Analysis on public access NHANES data to determine if there is evidence that elevated lead exposure may significantly contribute to renal disfunction and death.
Causal pathway: Lead Exposure -> Renal Injury -> Death.

Data Source:
United States. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, National Center for Health Statistics. (2012). National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES), 2005-2006. ICPSR [distributor], 2012-02-22. https://doi.org/10.3886/ICPSR25504.v5.
